Depression Assistant
Chatbot

Depression-Chatbot is an attempt to cure depression among the people by gradually understanding the root cause.​ It is developed from scratch with the intention of helping people in regulating their moods and eliminating distorted and negative thinking.​ This chatbot interacts with the user by analyzing and determine the input text for a certain emotion – anger, sadness, loss etc. and hence try to calm the person down. ​

Best Stock To Invest In

Predicting how the stock market will move is one of the most challenging issues due to many factors that involved in the stock prediction, such as interest rates, politics, and economic growth that make the stock market volatile and very hard to predict accurately. In this project, we have used technologies like NLP, we extracted meaningful information from unstructured texts such as financial news, developed an expert system that uses its knowledge base and inferencing techniques and indicators such as MACD and Stochastic to predict the best time to invest in Stocks.

Face Mask Detection

The year 2020 was a complete disaster for every living human being. Everything came to a stand-still and entire nationwide lockdown was announced. World Health Organisation provided guidelines and announced preventive measures. As per the guidelines, WHO mentioned multiple measures to be taken care of and wearing mask was one of them. Wearing masks proved to be the best precautionary measures to avoid the spread and it is now compulsory to wear it everywhere and in everything we do. Here we are as a part of responsible citizens presenting a system which will help to monitor, if everyone is wearing mask or not.
